Tuanfeng Y. Wang     ᶘ ᵒᴥᵒᶅ

I am currently a Research Scientist at Adobe Research. I got my PhD from University College London, advised by Niloy J. Mitra, and B.S. from University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Ligang Liu.

My research interests are computer graphics and computer vision, including neural rendering, geometry processing, computational fabrication, computational geometry, 3D reconstruction, augmented/visual reality, etc.

I'm regularly hosting Research Intern at Adobe Research. Please contact me if you are interested.
